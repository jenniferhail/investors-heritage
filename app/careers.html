We’ll treat you like family here.
Because to us, you are.

At Investors Heritage, employees are an important part of our success and we’re proud to have multiple generations of families who have worked for us or continue to work for us. We do our best to treat people the way we’d want to be treated. All we ask is for employees who the knowledge, skills, compassion, and desire to be successful in our service culture.
Investors Heritage is a smoke-free environment and an Equal Opportunity Employer.
